# FusionTwin Vision

FusionTwin is an open-source platform for building interactive digital twins of fusion reactors.

Our goal is to make fusion science accessible to everyone—from curious beginners to plasma physicists—through visualization, simulation, and experimentation.

## Core Principles

### Reactor Agnostic

FusionTwin should never be hard-coded for a specific reactor.

Tokamaks are our first implementation, not our only implementation.

### Physics First

Visualization exists to explain physics—not replace it.

Whenever possible, models should reflect real physical behavior while remaining approachable for learners.

### Progressive Learning

The same simulation should be useful to:

- students
- hobbyists
- engineers
- researchers

The interface should adapt to the user's desired level of depth.

### Open Science

Everything possible should remain open source.

We want FusionTwin to become a community platform for experimentation, education, and collaboration.

### Long-Term Goal

FusionTwin should become the easiest place in the world to explore, understand, and experiment with fusion reactor concepts.
